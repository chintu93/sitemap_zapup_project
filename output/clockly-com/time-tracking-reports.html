Everything you need to make informed decisions

Retrieve important analytics and get deeper insights into how your teams and projects are performing.

Productivity reports

View all the statistics that tell you how well your employees are performing, including automatically tracked hours, manually added hours, engagement levels and idle time. Choose date ranges to view your team’s overall productivity.

User dashboard

Have all of your own information available to you on dashboards for work activities and attendance. Know where you stand with your hours, earnings, and which apps and URLs you have visited so you can keep track of your statistics.

Team dashboard

Always know where your team members stand with their work and attendance data. Access team members’ information on engagement, working hours and earnings to see how they are performing and their attendance.

Filter & export

Take all of the information from your Track.ly reports and filter it to view the information you need. Export filtered reports as a .CSV file to share, save or store them in a universal format available to just about anyone.

Attendance reports

View your team's attendance reports for a bird's eye view of trends and specific details without having to calculate them or go through it one-by-one. Know who is working when and how many hours they have so you can allocate resources where they are needed.
