The kind of powerful impact that people talk about with respect to email marketing can only be created with accurate and valid email addresses.

Companies resort to a number of different techniques to find business email addresses on the basis of leads generated in different ways. The best way to find email addresses will be one that involves the least time and effort and at the same time offers the best outcome.

7 best ways to find email addresses

Use an efficient email finder tool

Email finder tools are one of the best most efficient ways to not only figure out email addresses but also to find the right people to contact. It is widely used by companies including some of the biggest names in different industries, which makes it a popular technique as well.

It offers dedicated features to help you find and contact the right people. Some of the best email finder tools come with browser extensions as well, with which you can simply view prospect company websites and have the extension crawl through to return valid email IDs of all important decision makers within the company. They also come with email verification tools that you can use to quickly verify the authenticity of email addresses, check for errors and ensure deliverability. This way it helps you clean out your email list and eliminate addresses that are incorrect, invalid or generic.

As is evident, email finder tools offer comprehensive lead generation features to help you improve your lead list in different ways.

Finder.io is the perfect example of an efficient email finder that comes with a Chrome extension to allow email verification on the fly. It offers domain search capabilities as well as finding capabilities that let you feed in bulk prospect information like first names and company names, and it will return a full blown list of valid email addresses to pursue. It also gives you ample context on the leads that you are targeting with its lead mining feature. This allows you to analyse the quality of your leads, and decide whether they are worth pursuing.

On top of all that it also comes with analytics that will help you make improvements as you go. This is why email finder tools are the best way to find email addresses.

Twitter’s advanced search tool

Twitter offers an Advanced Search Tool that not many people know of. It helps filter out the tweets made by a target person where they might have mentioned their email. In order to prevent bots from finding them, some people replace the symbols “@” and “.” with the words “at” and “dot”. Some people don’t even bother doing that. Either way, searching for unique words like “at” or “email” or “address” etc. with quotes will reveal tweets that use those terms, which is most likely going to be an email ID. Therefore, it serves to be one of the best ways to find email addresses of businesses.

Subscribe to the contact’s mailing list and send an email

Another effective method to find an email ID is to subscribe to the prospective client’s mailing list, by signing up through their opt-in form or through their social media pages. This will add to you to their marketing campaigns and you will most likely receive a welcome email, congratulating you on joining their family. It is of course an automated reply, but you can reply back with a question or a query and they are most likely to respond from a personal email address. This may not be the best way to find email addresses, but it is definitely worth a shot.

Directly ask for a personal connection through a contact form or through a generic email

Another effective tactic is to directly ask for a personal connection. If you visit the prospective company website you may come across a contact page with a form that you can fill in with a query. Use the form to put in your personal information and ask for a personal contact email ID directly. They will most likely respond to the email from a generic address like “customer care” or “info” etc. asking for a reason. Be sure to spell out a good reason. You can specify the “why” part in the contact form itself if you wish to, but if you prefer receiving their generic email ID too, you can always respond back specifying the reason. Most people will definitely oblige and give you an ID, as long as the reason is strong enough.

Simply reach out on Twitter

Twitter is one of the most widely used social media platforms, especially by business professionals. Hence, it offers a few best ways to find email addresses. Apart from the Advanced Search Tool mentioned earlier, you can also simply drop a tweet with the prospect person’s handle asking for the best way to contact them. It is a straightforward and direct way to obtain an email ID, and for the reason, businesses professionals will likely appreciate your effort and respond to your tweet. Make sure to be friendly, courteous and even fun in your tweet, as it would induce their interest. Some people may respond to your tweet asking for the reason, which you can give a vague idea about.

For example, a good and short tweet will be to mention the lead and say “what is the best way to contact you? Got a quick question.”

Search for the person on LinkedIn

On receiving a person’s first name or last name, one of the best ways to find their email address is to run a quick search on LinkedIn. Most professionals maintain a LinkedIn account and being a professional social media channel, some of them wouldn’t mind leaving their contact info on their profile.

Even without a readily available email ID, you can look up the person’s profile where they will most likely have mentioned the organization that they are working for. Find out the company and run a Google search to determine the right domain. You can then go to their contact page and drop a query as mentioned before.

You can also use the domain search tools that come with email finder software to help you with this. On entering the domain name, it will return a list of email IDs of all relevant people within the company. This is once again why it offers one of the best ways to find email addresses.

Use Content Explorer

Last but not least, you can make use of the Content Explorer tool on AHref. It is basically a rich library of informative articles, long form guides and other types of content authored by industry experts and published across a million websites on the internet. You can simply run a search on Content Explorer with a few keywords related to your industry and it will show you a whole list of articles on a variety of topics. It also shows you the names of the authors, which you can easily pull up through an export file as well. Once you have the first names and last names you can search on LinkedIn for the company name. You can also use an email finder tool to feed in the names and it will help you get to the right email ID. Above everything you can simply run a Google search with the info, which will help you discover a lot of details as well.

Apart from the above 7 methods, you can also simply make a guess sometimes and test it out. Figure out a few common email formulas or syntaxes and try your hand at filling up the blanks with the available information. Email permutation tools are also available to help you try out some commonly used formulas. If you are lucky enough, the email you are looking for could be one of them. It may not be the best way to find an email address, but it is a possibility.
