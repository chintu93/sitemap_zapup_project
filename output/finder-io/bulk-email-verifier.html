What is email verification?

Email verification or email validation is the process of checking whether an email ID is authentic, valid, accurate and deliverable. It involves checking the syntax, the domain, the validity of the address etc. and ensuring that an email to the ID will be delivered promptly.

A good majority of the email addresses that you receive through lead generation techniques like opt in forms or pop ups, for many reasons can be incorrect or invalid. The person entering their email ID could make a manual error, or they may use an email ID that they haven’t realized has gone bad. Or they may not wish to enter the correct ID for personal reasons. All of these can lead to an ineffective email list with addresses that will immensely bring down your deliverability and open rates.

This is precisely why you need to use email verification to clean your email list. It will help you carefully pick out the invalid or incorrect email addresses and ensure that your list contains only valid IDs. You can create a separate list of wrong email IDs to take further action.
