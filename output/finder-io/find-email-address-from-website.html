Outreach is perhaps the most vital and grueling part of the link building process. It becomes even more challenging if you are not equipped with the right tools and approaches. These not just enable you to find the email address of your target for link outreach, but save a lot of your efforts. Although link builders spend a lot of time in finding contact info, in many cases, this doesn’t return expected results.

Some of the most common challenge link builders usually face involves: - The contact page is missing in the targeted website - The contact page displays a generic email address - Too many email addresses with similar-sounding job titles- this will put you in a dilemma whom to reach out.

Whatever the challenge is, finding the right email address is important to any successful link outreach. Here are 6 quick ways to find email address for link outreach. Let’s start.

1. Test Your Best Guess

If you have the name of the person you want to email and the name of the organization where the person works, the easiest and quickest way is to execute the Try And Guess approach. Typically, every company formats its email addresses following certain patterns, and these patterns are limited.

Take, for instance, the name of your prospect is John Dev. Now the email variants of this name could be something like:

  • john@domain.com
  • john.dev@domain.com
  • dev.j@domain.com
  • john.d@domain.com

Don’t just start sending emails once you are equipped with these combinations. There are tools available that’ll help you figure out which one of these emails is the right one. Sales Navigator from LinkedIn is a notable name in this regard.

2. email search Tools

Another quick way to find the correct email address is to use email finding tools. These tools are highly powerful and include cutting-edge features, such as email finder, Domain Search, Mail Tracker, and email verifier. With these capabilities, you can quickly find the contact details of the person you want to reach out to.

Most of these tools come with both free and paid versions. The free versions don’t offer all the capabilities wherein with the paid version, you can enjoy all the fantastic features. You can always try out the free versions, and then based on the performance, opt for the paid ones.

One of the best notable email finding tool is Finder.io. It comes with the Google extension feature that you can install on your browser and get a much faster result while finding anyone’s email address.

3. Use Search Engines

Search engines are another useful resource to find email addresses quickly. Google, Bing, Yahoo, or any other search engine is a great place to find all sorts of information. All these search engines come with advanced search capabilities, that’ll help find email addresses for link outreach. The advanced algorithm of search engines is highly capable to track the hard to find web pages and hidden contact details.

Search engines index billions of websites, and when you run a search query, it quickly sifts through a huge database of websites at once and returns the webpage with the required contact details.

While searching for an email address, you may combine the target domain, name of the person, and specific keywords as search queries. This will help find the correct email address faster.

4. Leverage Social Media

Not getting success with search engines, or finding it time-consuming? How about directly contacting your prospect through any social media channel and ask for the contact details?

Due to the ubiquitous nature of social media, it’s expected that the person, whom you want to reach out, has a social profile either on LinkedIn, Twitter, or Facebook.

If you leave them a text, you might not get a response. So, it’s recommended to get connected with them by following their posts, shares, etc. in the respective channel before asking for the email address.

This process might be a bit slow comparing the other ways, but the chance of getting success is high and you can end up building a solid and long-lasting relationship.

5. Subscribe to the Newsletter

Most companies use email newsletters on a regular basis. You need to subscribe to these emails. This brings you an excellent opportunity to establish a relationship and find the email address for link outreach.

In many cases, these newsletters come with contact details of the respective stakeholder and you might get the email address of the person you want to reach out. If not, don’t worry. What you need to do is reply to the newsletter, appreciate their posts, and ask for the required contact details.

This method also comes with an added advantage. By subscribing to the newsletter, you’re already sending a message to the webmaster that you would like to build a relationship, which is very important for link outreach.

6. LinkedIn and Twitter Advanced Search Options

One of the quickest ways to find email address is to use the advance search options of LinkedIn and Twitter. You can simply head over to LinkedIn advanced search and type the name of the company that your prospect works for.

LinkedIn will return a list of relevant people who work in that company. If the company is big, the list will be extensive. Here you should include specific keywords or titles to narrow down the search.

In order to hide the email address from bots, many Twitter users include “dot” and “at” instead of “.” and “@”. You can bypass this by using the Twitter Advanced Search. Head to that section and search your prospect’s tweets for “at” and “dot”. You may also include words, such as email, reach, or contact to filter the results further.

Final Thoughts

Finding email addresses for link outreach is a challenging task. However, if you follow the aforementioned ways, you can easily get the correct email address of the person whom you want to reach out to very quickly. We recommend you to use all these ways wisely and you’ll definitely find it easy to establish relationships and build connections fast.
