Email marketing is a powerful marketing channel for sure. However, in order for it to work and unleash all of its much raved about benefits, there is a certain prerequisite – a good quality list of potential leads.

Building a good email list is one of the most challenging aspects of email marketing. Not just because of the strategic efforts involved in lead capturing and inducing visitors to fill out their contact information but also because of the cumbersome process of finding and validating accurate email IDs.

Lead generation as a whole is essentially all about finding the right email IDs. It is when targeted emails are created and sent to the right people with the right email IDs that the full power of email marketing is realized. And by right email ID, it doesn’t just mean ensuring a valid address for deliverability, but it is also about directing emails to the right decision makers within a company.

This is why it is extremely important to have the right email IDs. Sending emails to the wrong IDs and the wrong people can immensely affect click through rates, open rates and of course deliverability, ultimately standing the risk of being tagged as a spammer. As a matter of fact, pursuing the wrong person within an organization is not only a waste of time, but is a potential dead end in terms of conversion.

What is an email finder?

In simple terms an email finder is an online tool that helps you find valid and accurate email IDs of important people within a company. It provides features to upload basic contact information of individuals and companies in bulk, and returns a list of accurate email IDs with which you can directly contact your leads. Apart from email finding, they also provide other carefully designed features like email verifier tools, domain search tools and analytics to help you further in your lead generation activities. For convenience and ease of use, most solutions come with email finder extensions as well to boost efficiency.

Finder.io is one such feature packed email finder solution that offers efficient tools to determine accurate email IDs of the right people in a company. It lets you feed in either the first names or the last names or the domain names of potential leads in bulk, and returns 100% verified valid email IDs ready for you to contact. It also comes with an email finder extension for Chrome that lets you find and validate all associated email IDs of the company websites that you visit. You can either use the web app or the extension tool to find emails on the fly.

20 email finder Chrome extensions to check in 2020

Finder.io

Finder.io is one of the best most cost effective email finder extensions available today. With its highly accurate tools meant specifically for bulk email finding, verification and domain search, it offers the best value for money, especially for small businesses. It allows you to upload large lists of first names or last names or simply the domain names of captured leads in CSV or spread sheet file formats, and it returns an accurate list of email IDs belonging to the right decision makers in the lead companies. Needless to say it provides a Chrome extension to perform searches and checks on the go.

It also offers features for email verification which lets you validate the authenticity of the emails collected ensuring maximum deliverability and minimizing bounce rates. Its domain search tool will let you pull out reliable contact information for managerial decision makers within a company by simply feeding the domain name.

And lastly, it offers insightful analytics as well to help you implement data driven improvements.

Hunter.io

Hunter.io previously called Email hunter offers an excellent email finder extension and web app as part of its lead generation platform. It comes with an email verifier tool and a tracker for Gmail, and it lets you find accurate email addresses of relevant business professionals with just their first names or company websites. It allows for single as well as bulk searches and offers a freemium model with 50 free searches in a month.

Datanyze Insider

Datanyze Insider offers a unique feature wherein, using its email finder extension with browsers, you can simply highlight a person’s name as it appears online in any company website and it will return a set of valid email IDs for the name, highlighting the most likely one. Apart from that it also offers reliable email verifier features as well.

Find That Email offers one of the most accurate email finder extensions in the market. Headreach
