Smooth, Tailored Customer Onboarding Experience

Powerful customer onboarding software will help you improve your customers’ onboarding experience to increase adoption and engagement.

Personalized Emails

Filter and select users then quickly send personalized emails to them. Send relevant messages to users in specific locations, provide additional value to users who’re frequenting certain features, and more.

Predefined Conditions

Set conditions that’ll help you streamline your customer onboarding automation. And based on the condition’s outcome, send relevant emails, customer support tasks, and more.

Tasks

Assign customer support reps to assist users who meet certain conditions. Find out from your users themselves what’s blocking their success with your product, so you can optimize and improve.

Educate Customers

Assist users who’re on the verge of becoming inactive. Send timely emails to help them use features they were previously engaging with so they can get the most out of them. And create helpful product walkthroughs that’ll save you time and help your users with self-service.
