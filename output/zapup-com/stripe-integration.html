How Does it Work

Zapup automates your work by merging Triggers (like “New Customer”) and Actions (like “Create Customer”) to complete an action easily in one application while a trigger occurs in another application. Stripe integration lets you do your work smartly with series of actions like creating or updating customers that automatically execute as a result of the triggers.
