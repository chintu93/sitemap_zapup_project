The Story of Mailsend's Birth

Explore the inside story of why we built Mailsend, en email marketing platform and how it helps you achive better results in email marketing.

Email marketing plays an important role in engaging the audience and converting leads into customers. Although it has greatly evolved over the years, email marketing hasn’t lost its sheen at all. Instead, this age-old customer engagement tool still scores at the top of the list for generating sales.

However, to succeed with email marketing, the role of a full-fledged marketing automation solution is decisive. A sophisticated tool will help you create and send newsletters, manage drip campaigns from one central location, and connect with the audience like never before.

Initial Thoughts: How It Started

The success of Agile CRM, our all-in-one 500app’s customer relationship management (CRM) software helped us comprehend that we can help small and medium-sized businesses (SMBs) to enhance their marketing ROI in diverse ways. We realized that there are a number of processes that the marketing team performs every day don’t require manual intervention. We tried to find ways to automate these activities with available tools and technologies, but stalled by a number of challenges.

The Problem

When we started using the available Campaign Monitor tools available in the market, we noticed that there is no such tool that’s focused toward small and medium businesses and empower them to send emails like fortune 500.

Moreover, tools that combine features like crafting beautiful emails, creating automated journeys, and sending out personalized messages to improve relationships with customers are very expensive and beyond the reach of SMBs.

The requirement

Therefore, our research and development team conducted multiple brainstorming sessions and noted down the detailed requirements for a state-of-the-art email-marketing tool.

We wanted an enhanced email marketing solution that’ll boost open-rate, help us build newsletters, manage drip campaigns, and make data-driven decisions. Having all these features combined altogether is a major challenge for many organisations, which is why we consistently focused on building a single tool that addresses the needs of all email marketing needs for every business.

To be more precise, our requirements were as follows:

  • Build Outstanding Emails – Use drag and drop email editors or existing templates to create beautiful and responsive newsletters
  • Create Campaigns – Create, run, manage, and analyze drip campaigns at scale to drive engagement and improve the open rate
  • Email Automation – Automate your email campaigns with a visual email journey builder to send personalized emails on time and drive revenue

The Solution

Since there was no such tool available in the market featuring all these high-end features, we decided to build it on our own. And that is why Mailsend was built.

Mailsend: Key Features

Mailsend is highly capable of sending visually attractive newsletters by building effective drip campaigns that’ll enable marketers to connect with people at ease. Some of the key features of Mailsend are as follows:

  • Drag-and-Drop Email Editor – to create intuitive newsletters.
  • Form Builder – to build forms with real-time previews
  • Manage Newsletters – to upgrade your email communication
  • Contacts & Companies – to add, edit, and manage important contacts and company data
  • Analytics – to make informed decisions and optimize campaigns
  • Pre-made Templates – to build attractive newsletters with just a few clicks
  • Drips Campaigns – to create and run drip campaigns for maximum engagement
  • Automation – to personalize and automate emails and entire campaigns
  • Chrome Extension – to track and see how emails are performing on the go
  • Easy-to-Use Integrations – to quickly integrate with Sendgrid, Mailgun, Twilio account

  • Optimize your time and budget
