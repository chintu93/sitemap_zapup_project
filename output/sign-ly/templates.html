Avoid creating contracts all over again when you need to use typical documents. Skip entering manual data and turn your completed contract into a reusable template.

With templates, upload documents already on your computer, Google Drive or Dropbox, drag-and-drop fields you need signed and send!

Easily share your documents with your colleagues or customers for greater productivity. Create a link to the template you have built and share it with the other users.

Now that you have created an awesome template, you can safely store it by uploading it to the cloud storage of your choice. You can access it whenever you want.

Reusable Templates
