8 Best Customer Support Software Solutions Every Support Team Should Use

What is help desk software? Help desk software helps you stay on top of all your customers and employees needs through an intelligent system of tickets and integration. It’s a place where you can schedule alerts and build a knowledge base with the power of automation in order to better serve your clients. If you ever wonder about whether you are serving your customer to the best help experience you can possibly offer, then you might want to consider a customer support software solution that matches the needs of your business and customer. 

Some of the benefits of using help desk software are impressive since they include the ability to automate service, manage tickets, integrate with other software, create knowledge bases your team can use as reference, and the deployment of smart alerts. With these benefits of a help desk software solution, you can intelligently see that your customer is being helped to the best of your ability and that you are creating an experience that they will want to return to again and again.

Here is the top list of help desk software solutions:

  1. Support.ly Our top pick for this list comes from 500apps. It delights customers and drives growth through support tickets, knowledge bases, automation, and reporting that is as powerful as it is easy to manage. The feature rich support software is perfect for the startup, established business, and enterprise client in that it's feature-rich and a breeze to automate. You can even customize it with social media integration and canned responses for addressing customer concerns faster and automatically.

  2. Zendesk 

Zendesk is nothing if not efficient as it provides flexible ticket management, multichannel support, and advanced reporting. You can even build a knowledge base that allows you to build communities of helping professionals. The app fits the needs of most small businesses well, but it can also grow with you to be a full-scale enterprise solution to help desk support. 

  1. Freshdesk 

Freshdesk is simple, but powerful and comprehensive since it is built on an architecture that is truly intuitive. It has some of the best mobile apps for Android and iOS for help desk solutions, and it makes itself suitable for any business size and type (from freelancer to enterprise). You’ll immediately notice that it’s UI is neat and smooth, making it a breeze to integrate social media, deploy self-service, and manage all your tickets with automation. 

  1. Hubspot help desk software 

If you are part of the HubSpot ecosystem of sales, marketing, and CRM, you may want to consider integrating with their help desk as well. With automation and bots for all of your data, the service hub allows you to build complete customer service experiences that promote loyalty and happiness among your clients. You can use automation, conversational inboxes, tickets, and reporting through their help desk that was designed for small to mid-sized businesses. If you are an enterprise client, they can also help you create outstanding support environments. 

  1. LiveAgent 

LiveAgent is right there among the top players in the customer support software solution market. It has a comprehensive set of features on its multichannel support that allow you to pull together live chat, email, and social media to offer unforgettable help experiences. With features for gamification of support, web-embed contact forms, and a collaboration space, the app is great for small businesses and can scale to meet enterprise needs. 

  1. Zohodesk 

Zoho Desk is powerful. It has all the tools you need to turn your customer support process into one that improves on reporting and productivity. You can monitor all your agents and make data-driven decisions through its report features, and you can automate and support across channels with its automation abilities. All its capabilities together will lead you down the road to offering better customer relationships and increasing earnings overtime (not to mention loyalty). 

  1. Jira Service Desk 

One of the only open source solutions on our list, JIRA is great for helping teams collaborate on technical aspects of their projects. It helps you to see, find, and address bugs and system errors during development, testing, and launch. Through an intuitive UI, it runs a ticketing system as well as self-service portal and dashboard. For such teams, it’s a wonderful way to receive real time updates and metrics you need to measure the success of your service. 

  1. InterCom 

Intercom might be best known as a chat tool, but it’s customer support software solution features are as simple and easy-to-use as their chat solution. You don’t need too much to get started right way with helping customers since it is easy to get going with its collaboration and automation tools that were build to scale and improve customer support in your organization.
