Help your customers better with a self-serve library

Collect, organize and store content with knowledge base software features to allow for quick and easy editing, organization and adding of content.

Content Management

Generate and add instructional content for your customers to refer to before they submit a helpdesk ticket. Determine your knowledge base content based on feedback from customers and commonly experienced issues to provide a valuable self-serve library of reference material.

Drag and Drop

Drag knowledge base articles and drop them to organize your content lists any way you wish within the knowledge base software. Make finding the content easier for your customers and ensure they will find the answers to their questions quickly and avoid having to submit a helpdesk ticket.

Real-time Editing

Edit content any time updates or corrections are necessary. Edits can be made in either plain or HTML formats to make the process as easy as possible for your staff. Ensure your knowledge base has the most up-to-date information to help your customers help themselves.

Content Categorization

Categorize your knowledge base content in order to access the information more easily. Place content in intuitive categories in the knowledge base software, including author, section, descriptions, tags and more to make navigating through your knowledge base effortless for both staff and customers.
