Advantages and disadvantages of using a social media management tool

Discover the pitfalls to avoid when using social media management tools so that you can rake the most out of your social media campaigns.

For any business, the social media space is a lucrative and very appealing environment to not just do direct marketing but also build and enhance a brand. Successful social media campaigns are mostly conducted using social media management tools which offer a plethora of advantages. By using these tools, brands stay a step ahead of their competitors because these tools are not only effective but they also helps save a lot of time.

However, by using these tools and consequently increasing the visibility of their businesses, there are also some inevitable disadvantages associated with these tools.

In this elaborate post, we are going to go through both the advantages and disadvantages of using a social media management tool so that as a business owner who wants to harness the advantages of social media you can stay informed to effectively enhance your businesses visibility, presence and reputation online in order to improve the conversions and sales.

Advantages of Using A Social Media Management Tool

A Social Media Management Tool Can Help Increase The Online Visibility Of Your Brand

One of the core advantages of a using a social media management tool is that it effectively helps increase the visibility of your brand. The ease of use and efficiency in posting that comes with using a social media management tools heavily improves your consistency and builds trust with your users.

Under normal conditions, you would have to otherwise post manually, which costs you a lot of time and sometimes even money because you have to use the services of a human assistant. The best social management tools are the ones that take all this tedious work out of your hands and automate such tasks as making posts, responding to your clients and offering customer support.

Social Media Management Tools Effectively Help You Improve Your Online Reputation

A good online reputation is the basic foundation of any thriving business; especially if you are running it online. On the online space, people have the unique ability to leave you negative feedback which is visible to your other prospects thus damaging you and costing your financially.

However, by using a social media management tool to help you, you can build your reputation and even have that much needed advantage over your competitors. One of the ways you can do this using a social media management tool which automates your social customer service. With the right tool, you can respond to messages about your service or product on the fly without wasting any time or keeping your prospects waiting. This will not only cement your trust with them but it will also directly grow your sales positively.

The Right Tool Will Help You Scale Your Business

When you start a business, you want to grow and take it to new levels. For this reason, one of the things you should focus on is automating most of your tasks and delegating them at the minimum cost without compromising on efficiency.

This is where social media management tools come into play. With just a click of a button, you want to automate all your posts and schedule them without having to spend hours doing it manually. By taking the time off your hands, the social media management tool is helping you scale your business so that you can focus on other areas such as production or marketing.

Moreover, these tools when used well can help you save money which would have otherwise been used for hiring someone to do the posting for you, allowing you to allocate it to other departments of your business.

Social Media Management Tools Provide You With Useful Data

Now, like never before, and thanks to social media management tools, you are now able to determine how your posts are doing. This is one of the most underrated functions of a social media management tool. When you are able to view the data on your posts, you are able to know what your audiences and users like and what they do not like and thus you are able to tweak your posts accordingly. With factual data, you are even in a position to make better business decisions. This can be cemented by the words of former C.E.O of the computing giant Carly Fiorina who was once quoted saying that; the goal is to turn data into information, and information into insight.

You Can Know What Your Target Audience Wants By Using A Social Media Management Tool

When selling a product or a service, having the ability to know exactly what they want helps you sell to them better. The social media space is an ideal place to take the pulse of what your target audience demands and consequently get some feedback from your current users and clients. Using a social media management tool gives you the unique ability to listen to what your prospects want by conducting a survey or even by analyzing how your content is performing.

For example, if you brand includes a number of products and you often post image, the more engagement a product generates means the more popular it is. With an elaborate dashboard that presents this information to you in a palatable and easy to understand manner, you can focus on that product and maximize on your sales and returns. This is exactly the advantage a social media management tool presents you.

Demerits of Using Social Media Management Tools

As we have listed above, anything good usually comes with an inevitable downside. The same applies to social media management tools. Although these may not completely be deal breakers for you as a business owner, they are important to put into consideration. Below is a list of the most common disadvantages associated with using social media management tools.

Social Media Management Tools Require Skills To Configure and Operate

As with any other applications or software, you require some skills to effectively use social media management tools. Although these may be minimal, you need to know your way around the platforms to perform your tasks effectively. To solve this problem, you can spare sometime to train your staff using the elaborate manuals that come with the social media management tools.

Social Media Management Tools Require Some Investment

If you are a startup, you might find that you are engaging the use of many applications and its causing some form of financial burden before you even start recording any profits. But if you want to stay a a step ahead of your competition when running your social media campaigns, the use of a social media management tools is inevitable. Although it might cost some money, it is important to plan for a good social media management tool that will help ease your work and achieve the results you need.

Social Media Has the Risk Of Exposing You To Your Competitors

Social media management tools enhance your activities online and this can pose a risk for your business. This might work against you because in business you want to keep the cards that are the foundation of your success as close to your chest as possible. With this in mind, when you post about your products and your ideas online on your social media platform of choice, you inevitably risk exposing your secrets to your competitors. This is an inevitable risk, but you can counter the same by leaning more tricks of the trade by following your competitors and improving on their ideas.
