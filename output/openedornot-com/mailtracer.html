FAQs

Can I track my emails in real-time?

Yes! We’ll notify you every time an email has been read by the recipient. Your notification includes the subject of the mail, recipient details, and time.

Does Opened or not show read recipients?

Yes! If your emails have been read, it shows two green ticks whereas you’ll see grey ticks when your email is delivered.

Does Opened or not support other email clients?

Opened or not works seamlessly with Gmail. Currently, Opened or not is only available to download as a Gmail email tracker extension through Chrome store.

Is it possible to mute notifications?

Yes! Notification can be turned off if required.

Will I receive a daily report on sent emails?

Yes! We’ll send you daily reports about tracked email count, read/unread email count, and much more.

Can I monitor email replies using opened or not?

Yes! you can track email replies as well using Opened or not.

Can I track when someone opens an email attachment?

Yes, we can track if the attachment has been downloaded by the receipient.

Can I track emails with multiple receipients?

Yes, we can track if more than one person has opened the email.

Does Opened or not work with other browsers?

It is compatible with all chromium-based browsers such as Google Chrome, Microsoft Edge, Vivaldi, brave and many more.

Does Opened or not work with ad blockers?

Yes, you can use it with any ad blockers. You would need to add Opened or not to your ad blocker's safe/trusted sites list first.
