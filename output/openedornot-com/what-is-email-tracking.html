What is Email Tracking?

To the question what is email tracking the short and straight answer is email tracking enables a business or an individual to determine as to whether one’s email to people has been opened, the links clicked and the attachments viewed. What it essentially achieves is to give one the wherewithal to forge interactive relationships with one’s prospects in a highly competitive inbox scenario. According to David Newman, "Email has an ability many channels don’t: creating valuable, personal touches – at scale."

Essentially a method to track the delivery of email messages to the target audience in question, it involves the deployment of a digitally time stamped mechanism that is able to pinpoint the exact day and time of the receipt or opening of the email. It is also able to identify the IP address of the recipient.

**How does it function?

Email tracking makes use of tracking software that allows one to know exactly when somebody accesses our emails, clicks the links on view attachments. The software places a discreet image pixel in the email that allows one to determine the precise time and date an email has been opened by the recipient. That makes this technology very useful in determining whether the recipient of the email in question received it or clicked on the accompanying link. The information that can be gleaned from email tracking encompasses the IP address, the device and browser used by the recipient, as well as a date and time stamp pertaining to when the email was accessed.

**Why perform email tracking?

Now that we are clear about the answer to the question, what is email tracking, let's understand why we need to do it. There are several advantages that accrue from email tracking(https://www.hubspot.com/sales/email-tracking-guide). Let's look at what these might be-

*1. Helps Save Time-

It makes no sense for a marketer to send mail after mail to someone who doesn’t care to open it. By identifying such people email tracking makes it apparent to an email marketer that it is time to move on to more responsive prospects. This not only saves time and makes the emailing more efficient it also ensures that the brand prestige is maintained. This is on account of the fact that sending emails repeatedly to non responsive prospects is tantamount to spamming them. That is another answer to the question what is email tracking.

*2. Makes the Emailing Relevant-

By calibrating your emailing strategy based on the prospect data provided by email tracking you can ensure the relevance of your emailing. If, for instance, a prospect has opened your email a number of times, you can surmise that he or she has an interest in your offering. You can capitalize on that by sending a follow up mail. What’s great is that you can also determine if your follow up mail has been reviewed as well.

*3. Refine the Email List-

By enabling you to know as to who opens your email and who doesn’t email tracking allows you to prune your list of the people who are obviously not interested in your product or service. This will help you refine your list and make it more effective. It will also give you a better sense of what is email tracking.
