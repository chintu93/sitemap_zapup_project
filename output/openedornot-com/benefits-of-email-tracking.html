Benefits of Email Tracking

Email tracking is of great use to businesses, as it furnishes the right kind of data that allows businesses to make well considered business decisions. This is enabled by the ability of email tracking tools like OpenedorNot (https://openedornot.com) to obtain data pertaining to open rates, the times at which emails are accessed and the locations where this happens. They also help with the click-throughs on links as well as attachments. Armed with this kind of precise data any business will be able to hone and fine tune their email marketing in a manner that gets them optimal leads and customers. The benefits of email tracking don't really end and are quite a few.

Let us now take a look at how exactly does email tracking help a business:

**Saves one time-

By optimizing one's emailing abilities, email tracking saves businesses loads of time. If the recipients haven’t opened your mail the first time, they are hardly likely to do it if you send them follow up emails. Email tracking prevents this sort of thing from happening thereby saving an email marketer loads of time. That is one more of the benefits of email tracking.

On the other hand if someone has clicked on the links contained in a mail, you know that the person is likely interested in your offering and should be reached out to as this would be a timely move given the circumstances. In the highly competitive environment that a business has to operate in the ability to time ones moves, including reaching out just when a person is primed to buy is a prized one. Email tracking empowers a business to make very timely moves thanks to the precise analytics it provides about client behavior.

The time that email tracking software saves by enhancing the efficiency of outreach can be better utilized in undertaking more prospect outreach activities like increased email marketing. This sets a virtuous cycle of greater focused email marketing leading to a greater flow of leads leading to enhanced business. That is precisely what all businesses need in these hyper competitive times taking advantage of the tremendous benefits of email tracking.

**Provides context and insight-

Email tracking can help one refine both one's mailing list and one's messaging, helping one leverage email marketing better. This will be possible because email tracking furnishes a detailed history of the activities undertaken by a prospect. This gives one a pretty good insight into a prospect’s line of thinking. You may, for instance, find out what time of the day, or what day of a week a prospect is likely to open their mail. Now, isn't that a great benefit of email tracking.

You get a fair idea about the kind of subject lines that work and the ones that don’t. One could pretty much construct a social profile of the prospects. This lets you get very good at determining the best time to contact a prospect. The success or failure of any business are hinged upon their ability to make the right move at the right time. By providing context and insight about the email recipients, email tracking software enables just that. To a large extent it determines a business’s success or failure. Yet another one of the benefits of email tracking.

**Provides stellar analytics-

It provides the right kind of analytics in terms of open and click through rates providing one with the right kind of information to optimize results. Email analytics furnish huge amounts of data that can help improve the whole email marketing process. You can enhance your prospect engagement and earn their loyalty by smartly capitalizing upon the information available with you thanks to email analytics.

You can engage in sophisticated behavioral segmentation and connect with your prospects base in a way more personalized manner, enabling you to grow your business. This is one of the premier benefits of email tracking.

**Improves follow up-

Once you have precise mail open rates, you can initiate a targeted follow up. No longer do you have to rely on luck or chance to obtain leads. That is one heck of a benefit of email tracking.

On the contrary, you can be pretty certain that the people you follow up with are very inclined to respond positively to your mail. This makes the whole email marketing process very rewarding indeed and drives home the benefits of email tracking.

If email marketing has made a huge comeback, it is precisely on account of the fact that it enables targeted and effective outreach resulting in a very high incidence of business generation. Again, this is a great benefit of email tracking.

**Schedule emails to send them at the right time-

By knowing which days of the week are ideal for your kind of mailing you can draw up the right schedule. Email marketing works best when it is a planned, well thought out and calibrated exercise. Otherwise, it is no better than spam that just ends up antagonising one’s target audience. Anyone who doubts the benefits of email tracking should mull over that.

The best time to send out a marketing email is when the respondent is ready and conditioned to receive it in a positive frame of mind. Analytics provided by email tracking software let you plan your follow up emails with pinpoint accuracy and precision increasing your chances of growing your business manifold. The benefits of email tracking are simply too many to overlook.

**Improves Emailing Accuracy-

With a data based approach possible on account of email tracking, you are bound to improve email accuracy. The fact is that there are about 269 billion emails sent out and received every single day! As many as 40% of these are tracked, signifying the importance marketers attract to email tracking. They do that because they know that email tracking improves email accuracy, leading to a spectacular rise in ROI.

With every little information about the recipient available, including the device they use to receive the email, a savvy email marketer can very accurately profile their prospects in order of priority. Email marketers have extensive prospects databases to reach out to these days. Armed with the analytical information that they have been provided with thanks to email tracking, they can really leverage email marketing in a major way. That's another one of the benefits of email tracking you cannot ignore.
