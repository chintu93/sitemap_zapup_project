What is an AI Chatbot?

The first generation bots that many companies adopted were very rigid and provided poor user experiences. Rules-based chatbots are limited to very basic scenarios. On the other hand, AI-powered chatbots provide a more human-like experience, are capable of carrying on natural conversation and continuously improve over time. In simple terms, an AI Chatbot or artificial intelligence chatbot is a software that can spur conversation with the users in the normal/ natural language through various channels such as message applications, Website, mobile apps and telephones. It can converse with humans via audio or text. AI Chatbots are the smarter versions of chatbots. They can understand machine learning and natural language processing so that it can rectify human intent better.

Why are AI chatbots important?

Chatbots can deliver a functional interaction between your company and the customers, thereby enhancing the customer service experience. Moreover, it will improve the customer engagement process and operational efficiency by reducing customer service cost.

Selling online is tough. However, using AI chatbots for ecommerce can help you attract more visitors, engage with them, and keep your customers happy throughout their buying process. The greatest advantage of using AI chatbots for ecommerce is that they are available 24/7 and can provide the information your visitors need, exactly when they are looking for it.

If you run an ecommerce website, the right AI chatbot can help you:

1. Engage Your Visitors and Turn Them Into Leads

Everyone who visits your ecommerce website is a potential customer. As long as you have good products or services and great content, an AI chatbot can engage with your visitors and turn them into leads. Intelligent, AI-powered chatbots can chat with users and store their valuable contact information.

When a customer or potential buyer reaches out to your ecommerce business, your AI-powered virtual assistant can solve their problem or provide them with the right information. Pros and Cons of AI Chatbots

Advantages of AI Chatbots

There is no doubt that the demand for chatbot certification is increasing day by day. Some of the pros of AI chatbots are listed as below:

1. Increase sales

Chatbots can be utilized to provide instant support to your clients. This will further improve and enhance the sales generation of the business. You can also use artificial intelligence bots to do marketing of your products.

2. Availability

AI chatbots are available 24/7. With human client service, it is quite hard to offer 24/7 customer support service. AI chatbots can respond to each and every question that is prompted. This ensures that your client will get their queries solved whether it’s day or night.

3. Fast service

AI chatbots can quickly solve several issues as compared to human agents. Bots quickly search queries of customers online. They already have a large amount of repository. AI chatbots keep updated versions of repositories after each client interaction session.

4. Cost savings

You are required to pay your client support team every month, if you don’t use AI chatbots. AI chatbots also need some investment in the initial phase but in terms of long run, AI chatbots are much better. AI bots can automate your service processes and thus saves money.

Disadvantages of AI Chatbots?

Despite its advantages, Bot technology has also disadvantages. Sometimes chatbots fail to analyze voices in noisy areas.

1. Lack of feelings and emotions

A bot has no feelings or emotions. AI chatbots are able to manage several customer’s requests but they cannot manage when the conversations take an unpredicted turn. Sometimes it becomes critical for chatbots to interact in an effective manner since chatbots have no emotions.

2. Require maintenance

AI chatbots require continuous ongoing optimization, analysis and maintenance. You require to feed with an upgraded insightful information that they can utilize to respond to client’s questions and requests.

3. Difficult to create

Developing an AI chatbot from scratch is a very hard task. It requires significant effort and time. Although, using a chatbot tool like Botup can make the process much easier.
